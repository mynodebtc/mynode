Instructions to setup Sphinx Relay
  1. Ensure that Sphinx Relay is enabled and you see "Running" on the status tile
  2. Download and install Sphinx from sphinx.chat
  3. Paste the Connection String into the Sphinx application
  4. Sphinx should now be connected to your own node!
  5. Enjoy!
