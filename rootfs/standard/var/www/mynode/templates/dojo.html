Instructions to CONNECT Samourai Wallet to Dojo

  1. To complete the next steps, Samourai Dojo's Status must be "Running".
  2. You will also need Tor Browser, Dojo's V3 Onion Address and your NODE_ADMIN_KEY to access the Dojo Maintenance Tool.
  3. Open Tor Browser and copy & paste the V3 Onion Address provided above into the Tor Browser's address bar.
  4. You should now be on the login page of the Dojo Maintenance Tool.
  5. Copy your NODE_ADMIN_KEY into the input box and press enter.
  6. Once authenticated, you will be brought to the "Pairing" tab.
  7. In Samourai Wallet, go to the main wallet creation screen and select "Connect to existing Dojo" to connect to your Dojo back-end.
  8. Now scan the "PAIR YOUR WALLET WITH YOUR DOJO" QR Code.
  9. **NOTE** - Currently, you must create a new wallet to pair to your Dojo. To do this, you must first delete your current wallet.**See Instructions Below**
  10. Check your setup by going to "Networking" in your Samourai Wallet, you should see "Dojo Full Node" enabled with a green dot next to it.
  11. Congratulations, you are now your own bank!!

Instructions to DELETE Samourai Wallet

  1. **NOTE** - If you are going to delete your wallet, make sure you have your seed phrase and passphrase handy so you can restore your wallet after pairing.
  2. To delete your wallet, go to Settings -> Wallet -> "Secure erase wallet".
  3. On the main screen, to create a new wallet select "Connect to existing Dojo" and scan your QR Code.
  4. Once connected, press back and now go to the option "Import external wallet" and restore your wallet using your seed phrase.
